This website is about Formula 1 fan made website that lists the champions from 2020 all the way to 1990. There are information on the driver, car and picture of both. These drivers became champions as they were the best drivers or either their teams made the best car on the grid in the season they managed to become winners.

2020 Champion

The 2020 FIA Formula One World Championship was the motor racing championship for Formula One cars which marked the 70th anniversary of the first Formula One World Drivers' Championship. This season was dominated by mercedes winning the contructor title, the champion was thier driver lewis hamilton who comfortably won this season. His biggest opponent was his teammate Valtteri Bottas.
